Solution to the Cosmological Constant Problem

The cosmological constant problem - the 120 orders of magnitude discrepancy between predicted and observed vacuum energy - is resolved through the dynamic sequestering mechanism.

$$\Box \Psi + \lambda = \alpha \frac{2\Psi}{M_{\text{Pl}}^2}$$
